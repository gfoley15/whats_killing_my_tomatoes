PROJECT 4 OVERVIEW

Can a single image of a leaf determine whether your tomato plant is diseased? This is the question we aim to address in our course capstone project.

We plan to tackle this by developing two different machine learning models: one utilizing logistic regression and the other employing a neural network approach. Our goal is to enable users to upload images of their own tomato plants and utilize our models to assess their plants' health.
