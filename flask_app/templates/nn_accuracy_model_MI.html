nn_Tomatodiseases model

The nn_Tomatodiseases model relies on Neuro Networks to help in classification of tomato disease dataset. The model used 119,211 PARAMS for training and prediction with 100 EPOCH. Initial EPOCH accuracy started at 67% and kept improving as more iterations were added. Overall the model shows strong performance with an accuracy of 97%. This means using this model can classify unhealthy from healthy plant tissue images. Tune adjusting layers and activation were done to improve accuracy, however because of computation time, a higher order of tuning was not done. The few iterations tried gave an average accuracy of 97%. a review of loss across different EPOCHS showed a steady decline of loss from almost 62% to 10% by the time EPOCH 100 was reached
